## Reporting a Vulnerability
We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure.

If you discover a vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1. **Do not create a public GitHub issue.**  
2. Contact the XentoPay team privately via:  
   - **Email:** security@xentopay.com  
   - **Telegram (Admins Only):** https://t.me/xentopay  
3. Provide as much detail as possible, including:  
   - Description of the issue  
   - Steps to reproduce  
   - Potential impact  
   - Suggested fix (if any)

We will acknowledge your report within **48 hours** and provide updates as we investigate.

---

## Responsible Disclosure
We request that you:

- Do not exploit the vulnerability  
- Do not perform attacks that could harm users or funds  
- Do not publicly disclose the issue until it is resolved  
- Allow reasonable time for the team to deploy fixes or publish guidance  

Researchers acting in good faith will not be subject to any legal action.
